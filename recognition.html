Recognition

The Times magazine has recognized Linus Torvalds several times.
In 2000, Linus was ranked 17th in the Most Important People of the Century poll by Times magazine.
The Times magazine gave the title of “one of the most influential people in the world” in 2004.
The Europe edition of the Times magazine named him “one of the revolutionary heroes of the past 60 years” during 2006.
Linus Torvalds was presented with the 2000 Awards for Industry Achievement by InfoWorld.
During the summer of 2004, The Finish broadcasting company placed Linus 16th in the network’s “100 Greatest Finns”
